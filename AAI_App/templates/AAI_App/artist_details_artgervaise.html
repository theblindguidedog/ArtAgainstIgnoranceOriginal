Art Gervaise

Musician - Sydney

Art started learning guitar at the age of eight. He wrote his first half decent song at age twelve, which coincidentally, was a protest song about animal cruelty. He new at age thirteen music was going to be his life. He joined his fist band at fourteen and started calling the recording studio home at around the same time.

Art Against Ignorance was born out of a passion for writing and recording, a lifelong search for truth and the challenge of artistically combining the two.

"I can’t imagine a life without music. I can’t remember a time when music wasn’t there. I will do this for as long as I am mentally and physically able to. I always felt compelled to write songs that contained honest messages".

Art Gervaise
