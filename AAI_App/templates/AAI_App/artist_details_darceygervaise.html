Darcey Gervaise

Producer - Sydney

Darcey Gervaise is a NSW South Coast based producer, engineer and songwriter who has worked across many genres and artists including, Pacific Avenue, TEX, El Grande, The Tin Knees and Sam Allen.

Darcey began his songwriting career with his band The Zilzies, going to release a number of songs amounting to over a million streams. This is where Darcey and his brother Zach began SLM Productions, a studio and rehearsal space located in North Wollongong.

Of late, the young, promising producer/songwriter Darcey Gervaise has begun working with Echo & Salt and Waratah Records for management.
