ART AGAINST iGNORANCE

The purpose of ART AGAINST iGNORANCE is to help educate, inform, enlighten and bring awareness. My goal is to establish the label as a symbol of science, free thought, critical thinking, progressive thinking, empathy, compassion and unity. AAi is conscience driven rather than consumer driven.

In our technological paradigm of rampant commercialism and viral misinformation, I believe art and media share a responsibility to convey messages that are honest and altruistic rather than contrived and selfish. Throughout history art has been fundamental in communicating ideas and information. Reaching people intellectually by helping to arouse their emotions.

My hope is that AAi will evolve into a platform where like minded artists, influencers, communicators and others from the community meet up, exchange ideas, form relationships and collaborations.

ART AGAINST iGNORANCE is a fusion of two things, my lifelong love of music and my ongoing search for truth. It is about so much more than just art. It is about being thought provoking. It is about working in unity with others towards common goals. And most of all it is about doing all we can to inspire real and positive change.
